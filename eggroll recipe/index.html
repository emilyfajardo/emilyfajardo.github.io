Chicken Egg Rolls

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • ginger
  • Shredded Cabbage
  • Shredded Carrots
  • 8 eggroll Wrappers
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Chop Vegtables
  2. Cook Chicken in a pan
  3. Add in soy sauce
  4. Add in Vegetables
  5. Wrap in egg roll wrapers
  6. Fry in vegetable oil
